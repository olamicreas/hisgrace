Answers You Need

Your Top Home Care Questions (FAQs)

Weve compiled the most common questions asked by families seeking reliable home care in Lithonia and surrounding areas.
Contact Us
How do I know which type of home care my loved one needs?
We start with a simple consultation to learn about your loved ones condition, daily habits, and long-term care goals. Then we build a personalized care plan that matches their needs, lifestyle, and family preferences, whether they need companionship or full-time assistance.
Are your caregivers qualified, trained, and background-checked?
Yes. Every caregiver at HIS GRACE HOME CARE LLC is carefully screened through background checks and hands-on interviews. They are also certified or trained according to state requirements and regularly evaluated for performance, safety, and professionalism.
Can we change or adjust care hours later on?
Absolutely. We understand that care needs evolve. Whether its adding overnight support, adjusting weekly hours, or switching to live-in care, our flexible scheduling makes it easy to update services with a simple call, no penalty or delay.
Do you accept Medicaid for transportation in Lithonia?
Yes, we do. We offer private-pay rides and Medicaid-funded transportation services. If your loved one qualifies under Georgias Medicaid program, well help guide you through eligibility paperwork and all the coordination required for approved non-emergency trips.
